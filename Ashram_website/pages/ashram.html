Shri Siddheshwar Baba
(Dr. B.S. Goel)
(The following lines have been taken from ..........)
Right from 1975,when my Third Eye opened and kundalini got activated,I kept on getting powerful mental waves off and on to start some kind of ashram which should in times come,emerge into a centre for doing pyscho-spiritual and religious work.With passage of time,I ceased to see any purpose of my life except this.It was clear to me that an ashram was going to be established some day and further it was going to be one of the main centres of religio-spiritual work which would be established by the disciples of Bhagwan Sri Sathya Sai Baba in various parts of country from where spiritual waves would rise in the future .

In the first week of Januray 1982,a sadhaka came up with a suggestion of forming a registered society and thus Third Eye Foundation of India was founded.The name was preffered for three reasons.Firtly it represented a truth about my experiences. Secondly,in my books i have often described Baba as "Third Eye" of the whole universe who sees everything,everywhere,every moment. Thirdly,it was not my desire to create any confusion about the organisation and management,resulting in any kind of conflict with persons working in various organisations of Baba.

I knew perfectly that His Will is behind all the work.I was absolutely clear in my mind that the institution was not being built to compete with anyone,least of all with Sri Sathya Sai Baba organisations.To take a crude example,it would be a shop of a beloved son to sell the goods manufactured by the Father in order to extend the sale of goods with the inner permission of the Father.Although the ashram would be raised by the visible body of Dr.B.S.Goel and certain devoted sadhakas,yet it was obvious to me that in coming times it would be recognised as creation of Bhagwan Sri Sathya Sai Baba.So as far as financial support is concerned He would Himself see to it.

While preparing the draft of institution it was tentatively decided that we should consult one or two documents of Registered Societies and Trust.Thus the Memorandam of association of "Kingdom of Sathya Sai" was kept on mind while framing the document. Finally the society was approved and registered on June15,1982.

It was then that a Land Acquisition Committee was formed by Managing Committee of Third Eye Foundation of India for specific purpose of selecting and purchasing a good piece of land for ashram.Right from the day of its constitution,the committee visited many places like Rishikesh,Garhmukteshwar,Baghpat,Bijnor,Meerat etc but nothing concrete happened.While we were not succeeding in geeting suitable land for the ashram an increasing no of sadhakas were getting dreams about the ashram and work going on.

I sent an application to my office in september 1985 for a lonf leave of about four and a half months with clear intention that i would not join the office again unless forced by some rules to do so.Instead i would seek voluntarly retirenment.During this period of leave i would sit home and do some religio-spiritual work on lines i was already doing and wait for aquisation of land in which case i would start the work for the ashram otherwise i would seek a kutiya(hut) on the bank of river Ganga in Rishikesh and shift to the hut thus vacating my office residence in delhi.

Will i change my name and dress in accordance with tradition of yogis who put saffron clothes? During last so many years various sadhakas have addressed me with different names.Some of these were Nathji,Guriji,Swamiji,Jnanamurthy Dr.B.S.Goel.Most of the people addressed me as Guruji.Many of sadhakas also suggested me to change my name to Sathyanandaji or Jnanaanandaji.But i knew in my heart that these all names donot represent the inner reality or truth.So i decided at heart to let all sadhakas call me whatever they like.As far as my clothes were concerned i decided not to change my traditional clothes whih i used to wear at office(western dress) or at home(Kurta-pajama) for i clearly knew that body was false.The most obvious thing i want to convey here that the external names,clothes and other paraphernalia like rosary etc. have no relationship with the inner realisation I also at heart decided that whenever such an ashram comes up i would depend on my earning which would accure to me from my pension etc.While i would give everything to ashram that i can,i would never take anything from it for my living.
