Magical Words of Siddheshwar Baba(Guruji)
“Bad qualities are soldiers of ignorance and Good qualities are soldier of wisdom or awareness”.
“Spirituality is swimming against the current”.
“God is abstract but Guru is tangible”.
“Good is good bad is bad”.
"This world is a projection of God . It emerges from God and merges back into God" .
“Godward path is the highest path” .
“He who can conquer desire for physical pleasure, can conquer death”.
“Animal (nature of man) must be killed to make room for (development of) spiritual man”.
“When a spiritual Sadhak has vanquished all his senses appetites , his body become a pure Â and proper vessel to hold Atma”.
“No saint attained perfection without complete purification of his mind”.
“Even the slightest temptation on the path of Sadhna is a test of your moral strength steadfastness and preservance . If you fail, you fall; Therefore be vigilant”.
“There is a hidden and innate power in chastity”.
“Purification , illumination ,and perfection are three states On The Spiritual Path. It is the path that leads one from senses to the soul. Only he can tread this path,  who can declare war on desires, one has to crucify the lust of flesh”.
“The soul loves purity. He who is ever pure, reaches the goal”.
“Fascination by the body is the death of liberation of a seeker”.
“As the dawning of the day is simultaneous with the passing of night, search the dawning of true knowledge simultaneous with the passing of Desire”.
“He, who rejoices in the objects of senses and passion is like a thirsty man, who drink poison to quench his thirst”.
“Divinity starts unfolding inside a man as soon as he throws out Desire”.
“To attain perfect purity, one has to become absolutely passion free in thought, speech and action”.
“Views can be effective only when they are the outcome of an inner conviction”.
“Only when the tumult of the senses is stilled can the external wisdom be revealed to one, who seeks mystic communion with God”.
“Without the grace of a true Guru, it is utterly impossible to attain atma-gyan or self-realization”.
“No one can serve the flesh and spirit simultaneously”.
“Like the light of day and darkness of night, as both merge in Twilight ,so all preferences and abstinences must merge in Atambhav”.
“Mark!  any act that is not perfectly pure will lead to spiritual suicide”.
“Unless a person is absolutely chaste,  he should not hope for salvation. Blessed are the undefiled, sense gratification renders one utterly unfit for spiritual life”.
“Over-powering fascination by the body must be rejected resolutely to get admission in the courtyard of your beloved Isht”.
“You are merely a witness in this pulsating play of creation, you are an unconditioned seer of this cosmic drama, you are ever pure, birthless and deathless spirit”.
“A guileless, humble and simple person is God's Beloved”.
“When, a thought holds on to an object, it is bondage. Reject all imaginative thoughts. Fantasies can drag you into the foulest mud”.
